# Boba Rush

Race with your friends to make boba drinks! But beware: put too much caffeine and you might kill your customer! Create a room and invite your friends or join one with a room code. Once a round starts, click on the ticket in the top right corner to view the order. Adjust the sliders to mix together the correct flavor, drag the scoop to add boba pearls, stop the dial on the correct caffeine level, and go as fast as you can! Who will come out on top as the best boba barista?
